What is this?

This site finds your location and shows it on a map along with the locations of any commemorative plaques nearby.

Why?

It was built as part of @historyhackday 2011 by @fredkingham and @grahamlyons and the data comes from the Open Plaques project; it's fun to know which important, historical people have been near where you are right now!
